About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2070 BS by a team of two dedicated individuals, our medical wholesale business started with the goal of supplying high-quality medicines at reasonable prices. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted medical suppliers in Chitwan, providing a wide range of medicines and vaccines.


Our Mission

Our mission is to supply Nepal with top-notch medications and first-rate service. We are pleased to provide reliable healthcare across the country by serving not only Chitwan but also Birganj, Kathmandu, Gorkha, Itahari, and other important areas.


Why Choose Us?

We are Nepal's trusted medical wholesaler, providing quality medicines at fair prices.

During the pandemic, we ensured essential medicines remained available, proving our dedication to healthcare.

We prioritize customer satisfaction by valuing feedback and improving our services.

We ensure patients get the medicines they need on time, never leaving them without options.

Our Partnered Pharmaceutical Companies

Sun Pharma

The biggest pharmaceutical firm in India and a world leader in reasonably priced generic medications is Sun Pharma. Through its subsidiaries such as Ranbaxy, Selecta, Akuna, Arian, Azura, Symbiosis, Synergy, and Eye Care, it offers therapies for issues related to the brain, heart, mental health, and eyes.

Cipla Ltd.

Cipla is well-known for its cutting-edge treatments for heart disease, HIV/AIDS, and respiratory issues. Its primary and specialized businesses, such as Cipla Ltd. (1), maintain its focus on providing inexpensive healthcare globally, catering to both hospitals and retail establishments.

Abbott Healthcare Pvt. Ltd.

Abbott is a multinational healthcare corporation that is well-known in India for its thyroid, diabetes, and women's health medications. Through subsidiaries like Megalead, it provides both prescription and over-the-counter medications to clinics and hospitals nationwide.

Intas Pharmaceuticals Ltd.

Intas is a rapidly expanding international business that specializes in long-term therapies, sophisticated biologics, and cancer care. With divisions like Alecta, Altima, Aquila, Suprima, Vector, Viva, and Zenith, it provides a robust selection of hospital and specialty medications with a broad market reach.
